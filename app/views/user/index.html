    Tell us something in confidence

    Use this service to report criminal or suspicious activity such as:

    • betting in pubs and illegal gaming machines
    • concerns about money laundering
    • underage gambling
    • concerns about sports betting integrity (match-fixing)
    ! Warning Do not use this service to report a crime in progress, you should call the emergency services instead.

    You can make a report anonymously - you don't have to give your name or contact details unless you want to.

    Start now

    What you need to know

    What happens after you report suspicious activity

    The intelligence team will look at the information you give. They won't be able to tell you the outcome of their investigation.

    You can also report suspicious activity by telephone.

    Telephone: +44 121 230 6655

    Crimestoppers

    We work in partnership with the charity Crimestoppers as part of an ongoing strategy to protect against match-fixing, money laundering and illegal gambling.

    Crimestoppers will pass on reports made to them about gambling-related criminality or corruption to our Sports Betting Intelligence Unit (SBIU) at the same time as they are reported to the police.
